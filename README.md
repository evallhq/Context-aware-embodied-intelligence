# Context-aware embodied intelligence: From environmental understanding to autonomous decision-making

**中文标题**: 上下文感知驱动的具身智能: 从环境理解到自主决策

**Authors**: Hanqian Luo, Jiannong Cao, Xiuxiu Qi, Rui Cao, Yongchun Fang

**Published in**: SCIENTIA SINICA Informationis (2026, Vol. 56, No. 2, pp. 277-295)

**Official Article / DOI**: [https://doi.org/10.1360/SSI-2025-0245](https://doi.org/10.1360/SSI-2025-0245)

## Abstract

In recent years, embodied intelligence has emerged as a significant research direction in artificial intelligence, aiming to enable an agent to autonomously achieve intelligent behaviors in complex environments through the integration of perception, interaction, and decision-making capabilities. This research area not only focuses on the computational processes of internal algorithmic models within the agent but also emphasizes the physical structure of the agent and its real-time interaction with the external environment as core elements to the emergence of intelligence. Context awareness introduces a novel research perspective to embodied intelligence, serving as a key driving force that empowers the agent to understand environmental semantics, adapt to dynamic changes, and autonomously execute efficient decision-making. On this basis, this paper provides a systematic review of recent advancements in embodied intelligence, focusing on aspects such as environmental understanding, cognitive reasoning, and autonomous decision-making. In addressing the challenges of real-time semantic understanding of multi-source sensory data and continuous decision-making in long-term tasks, this paper further analyzes how the emerging paradigm of context-aware embodied intelligence, centered on perception-action closed-loop frameworks, relies on effective context-aware modeling and reasoning to adapt autonomously to dynamic environments and task constraints. This framework enables the agent to achieve more efficient and contextually aligned task execution in real-world scenarios. Additionally, this paper explores remaining challenges and outlines potential future research directions in embodied intelligence.

## 摘要 (Chinese Abstract)

近年来, 具身智能作为人工智能领域中的重要研究方向, 旨在通过感知、交互和决策能力的融合, 使智能体能够在复杂环境中自主地实现智能行为. 此研究领域不仅关注智能体内部的算法模型计算过程, 更将智能体的物理结构特性与外部环境的实时交互视为其智能产生的核心要素. 上下文感知为具身智能领域提供了新的研究视角, 成为赋能智能体理解环境语义, 适应动态变化并自主作出高效决策的关键驱动力之一. 基于此, 本文从环境理解、信息认知推理和自主决策等方面系统性地梳理了当前具身智能领域的研究进展. 针对智能体需要对多源感知数据进行实时语义理解和在长期任务中进行持续决策的挑战, 本文进一步分析了基于上下文感知的具身智能感知–决策闭环框架的新范式如何依赖于有效的上下文建模和推理来自主适应动态环境和任务约束, 使智能体能够在现实世界中实现更高效、更符合上下文的任务决策. 此外, 本文还探讨了具身智能领域中的其他挑战以及未来可能的研究方向.
